About MR HATS

San Antonio's Trusted Hat Destination

MR HATS is a family-owned hat store located in the heart of San Antonio, Texas. We specialize in premium western hats, modern caps, and everything in between. Our knowledgeable staff is passionate about helping every customer find their perfect fit.

Whether you're looking for a classic Stetson for a rodeo, a New Era cap for game day, or a stylish felt hat for a night out, we've got you covered. We carry trusted brands like Stetson, Resistol, Ariat, Bailey, Charlie 1 Horse, Larry Mahan, and New Era.

Come visit us in San Antonio and experience the MR HATS difference. Expert fitting, competitive prices, and the best selection in South Texas.
